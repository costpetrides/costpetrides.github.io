Welcome!

Here you’ll find notes and exercises for each class. To get the most out of your studies, first read the notes carefully, then try to solve the exercises on your own, and finally check the solutions to confirm your answers. Memorizing formulas isn’t necessary—the key is to understand the physics behind each problem. In Physics, it’s especially important to fully grasp each concept before moving on to the next. Think of it like building a house: without a solid foundation, the house could collapse.
